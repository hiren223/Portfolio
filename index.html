I'm a Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) graduate from Silver Oak University, Ahmedabad, with a growing focus on data science, statistics and building models that actually work on messy, real-world data.

As my final-year graduation project, I co-built Krushi Manch, a multilingual agricultural web platform — my first real taste of taking a product from requirements to a working system with a 4-person team.

After graduating, I completed a 6-month Data Science Consultant internship at Rubixe, where I worked across proof-of-concept and client projects — cleaning data, engineering features, training and evaluating models, and learning to read a confusion matrix as carefully as an accuracy score. I followed this up with certifications from NASSCOM FutureSkills Prime and IABAC.

My goal: keep building toward a full-time career in data science, analytics and applied AI — one dataset at a time.
